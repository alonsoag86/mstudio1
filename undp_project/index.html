Neonatal and Under Five Mortality Rate in Nigeria
According to UNICEF, global deaths of newborn babies remain alarmingly high. This trend is particularly true in low-income countries. Sub-Saharan Africa is the region that registers more cases of neonatal deaths around the world and Nigeria is one of the countries of major concern. With the newborn mortality rate of 29 deaths per 1,000 births, the global estimates rank Nigeria as the 11th highest on newborn deaths. Nigeria is also one of the six countries that account for half of the global under-fve deaths. The following project aims to explore if there is a relationship between access to electricity, ARI symptoms, sanitation facilities, improved water sources and the neonatal and under five mortality rate in this country.
